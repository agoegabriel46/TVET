Course Description

Welcome to the captivating world of scientific discovery! The "Exploring Science Through Experiments" course is designed to ignite the curiosity of young minds through hands-on experiments, interactive activities, and engaging discussions. Throughout this course, children will explore various scientific concepts, develop critical thinking skills, and cultivate a love for the wonders of the natural world.

What Are Pick Up And Drop Off Times For My Child?

Students may arrive 15 minutes prior to the scheduled start time and they must be picked up no later than 15 minutes after the end time. Unfortunately, we do not offer extended care beyond these times for our Children woodworking classes.

Full (100%) refund if canceled at least 21 days in advance of the start date, half (50%) refund for cancellations 14 days in advance of the start date, and no refunds less than 14 days in advance of the start date. Summer camp: Full (100%) refund if canceled.

Introduction To Scientific Inquiry

  1. Understanding the scientific method: observation, hypothesis, experimentation, analysis, and conclusion.
  2. Introduction to safety procedures in a laboratory setting.
  3. Exploration of different branches of science: biology, chemistry, physics, and environmental science.

Chesmistry For Children

  1. Basics of chemistry and the properties of matter.
  2. Conducting simple chemical reactions with safe household items.
  3. Introduction to the periodic table in a kid-friendly manner.
  4. Hands-on experiments demonstrating concepts like states of matter, chemical changes, and reactions.

Biology Adventure

  1. Discovering the world of living organisms: plants, animals, and microorganisms.
  2. Hands-on dissections and observations of plant and animal specimens.
  3. Learning about ecosystems and the interdependence of living organisms.
  4. Exploring biodiversity through interactive activities and games.

Earth and Space Exploration

  1. Exploring the Earth's geology, weather, and atmosphere.
  2. Investigating celestial bodies and the wonders of space.
  3. Constructing models of the solar system and understanding planetary movements.
  4. Hands-on activities related to astronomy, meteorology, and environmental science.

Creative Science Project

  1. Collaborative project work where students apply their scientific knowledge.
  2. Designing and conducting their own experiments with guidance.
  3. Presentation of projects to peers, encouraging effective communication of findings.
  4. Reflection on the scientific process and the importance of curiosity in learning.

Join us on this exciting journey of scientific exploration, where young scientists can unleash their creativity and curiosity to uncover the mysteries of the world around them!

Course Description

Welcome to the exciting world of robotics! This hands-on and engaging course is designed specifically for Children to explore the fundamentals of robotics, programming, and problem-solving. Throughout the program, young learners will embark on a thrilling journey that combines creativity, logic, and teamwork, providing them with a solid foundation in robotics.

What Are Pick Up And Drop Off Times For My Child?

Students may arrive 15 minutes prior to the scheduled start time and they must be picked up no later than 15 minutes after the end time. Unfortunately, we do not offer extended care beyond these times for our Children woodworking classes.

Full (100%) refund if canceled at least 21 days in advance of the start date, half (50%) refund for cancellations 14 days in advance of the start date, and no refunds less than 14 days in advance of the start date. Summer camp: Full (100%) refund if canceled.

Introductio To Robotics

  1. Understanding the basics: What is a robot?
  2. Exploring different types of robots and their applications in real life.
  3. Safety guidelines for working with robots.
  4. Introduction to robot components: sensors, actuators, and microcontrollers.

Building Your First Code

  • Hands-on experience in assembling a simple robot using a kit.
  • Introduction to basic tools and their usage.
  • Understanding the role of each component in the robot's functionality.
  • Troubleshooting and problem-solving during the assembly process.

    • Programming Fundanmentals

    1. Introduction to block-based programming languages suitable for Children.
    2. Writing simple programs to control the robot's movements and actions.
    3. Understanding sequences, loops, and conditional statements.
    4. Debugging and testing programs to ensure proper functionality.

    Sensors and Perception

    1. Exploring different sensors such as light sensors, ultrasonic sensors, and touch sensors.
    2. Integrating sensors into the robot for environmental perception.
    3. Understanding how robots gather information from their surroundings.
    4. Hands-on projects to demonstrate the practical use of sensors.

    Final Project and Showcase

    1. Collaborative project work where students apply knowledge gained throughout the course.
    2. Designing and building a creative robot with specific functionalities.
    3. Presenting final projects to parents, showcasing the skills learned.
    4. Reflection and discussion on the importance of robotics in various fields.
    This robotics course for Children aims to spark curiosity, creativity, and a passion for learning in the fascinating world of robotics. Join us on this adventure, where innovation meets fun!

Course Overview

Welcome to "Introduction to Electronics for Children" – an engaging and interactive course designed to spark curiosity and creativity in the world of electronics. This course is tailored for young minds, aiming to make learning about electronics fun, accessible, and hands-on.


Course Description

In this course, Children will embark on a thrilling journey through the fundamental concepts of electronics. From understanding what electricity is to building their own simple electronic projects, participants will gain a solid foundation in the fascinating realm of circuits, components, and innovation.

What Are Pick Up And Drop Off Times For My Child?

Students may arrive 15 minutes prior to the scheduled start time and they must be picked up no later than 15 minutes after the end time. Unfortunately, we do not offer extended care beyond these times for our Children woodworking classes.

Full (100%) refund if canceled at least 21 days in advance of the start date, half (50%) refund for cancellations 14 days in advance of the start date, and no refunds less than 14 days in advance of the start date. Summer camp: Full (100%) refund if canceled.

Introduction to Electronics

  1. Understanding the basics: What is electronics?
  2. Safety guidelines for working with electronic components.
  3. Introduction to common electronic components: resistors, capacitors, LEDs, and switches.
  4. Overview of circuit diagrams and their symbols.

Building Simple Circuit

  1. Hands-on experience in building basic circuits.
  2. Understanding the flow of electricity in a circuit.
  3. Exploring series and parallel circuits.
  4. Introduction to breadboarding as a tool for prototyping.

Electronic Components and Their Functions

  1. In-depth exploration of various electronic components.
  2. Understanding the role of transistors, diodes, and integrated circuits.
  3. Experimenting with sensors like light sensors, temperature sensors, and motion detectors.
  4. Building projects that incorporate multiple electronic components.

Introduction to Microcontrollers

  1. Overview of microcontrollers and their applications.
  2. Introduction to programming microcontrollers using block-based languages.
  3. Hands-on projects using microcontrollers to control lights, sounds, and motion.
  4. Understanding the connection between software and hardware in electronics.

Final Project And Showcase

  1. Collaborative project work where students apply their knowledge to create an innovative electronic device.
  2. Designing, building, and programming a unique gadget.
  3. Showcasing final projects to parents, highlighting the skills learned throughout the course.
  4. Reflection and discussion on the potential applications of electronics in various fields.

Join us on this exciting journey into the world of electronics, where young inventors can turn their ideas into reality and discover the magic of electronic gadgets!

This course is suitable for curious minds aged 6 - 16 who have an interest in understanding the world of electronics. Whether your child is a budding scientist, engineer, or simply loves tinkering, this course will provide a solid foundation for future exploration and learning.
